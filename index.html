About

Lynbrook's National Chinese Honor Society holds weekly meetings immersing members in the Chinese culture by holding fun events along with the informative presentations. With inter-club socials, a ping-pong tournament, Chinese tutoring, and more, NCHS welcomes all students to learn more about China. All students, regardless of ethnicity or second language, are welcome to apply.

We meet every Tuesday brunch in room 104 (Ms. Chen's room), starting 9/11.

Our meetings usually consist of cultural presentations and announcements for upcoming activites. Several activities we held previously are: a Chinatown day trip, dumpling making socials, karoake, and ping-pong tournaments. In addition, our tutoring program offers experienced members a chance to tutor people in the community for pay.

Applications

All members must submit an application, except for returning members from last year with over 35 points (an active member essentially). The 2018-19 school year application is at tinyurl.com/NCHS1819 and is due on 9/18 to any officer. Follow the instructions on the doc.

Points

Members earn points by attending meetings, volunteering, attending activities (socials and other events), etc. Point requirements and point sheets will be announced soon.
